Action Plan for the Next 7 Days
• Cut waste first. Reduce or pause campaigns with spend and no sales, starting with Cat Cube Auto 
Substitutes, Tunnel Auto Close Match, and Tunnel Auto Substitutes.
• Harvest proven search terms into exact match campaigns. The clearest signals in this file are b0dz449831, 
mesh cube for cats, cat cube, and the converting ASIN terms.
• Lower bids on weak auto and broad traffic. The account cannot afford to keep buying clicks at current 
conversion levels.
• Protect the winners. Keep budget flowing to the bundle campaign and the exact cat cube campaign since 
those are carrying the account right now.
• Treat Sponsored Brands as a low priority until Sponsored Products is efficient enough to support 
expansion.
Final Recommendation
Do not scale this account broadly yet. Tighten search term control, shift spend into the few campaigns 
that are already converting, and stop paying for curiosity clicks. This account has upside, but right now 
efficiency discipline matters more than expansion.
